    Julie Durda Meteorologist

    Durda started her career at KXTV and she then worked as an anchor for the traffic and weather at KPHO station. She covered the Pacific Hurricane season and this included the monsoons. Furthermore, Durda has worked for several stations like WSVN, WPLG, World News of ABC, Good Morning America. Additionally, she was a member of the American Meteorological Society (AMS).

    In the year 2006, Julie Durda became a member of the National Weather Association (NWA) and also part of the CBS Storm Team in Phoenix. In 2011, she earns the honor as the Best Meteorologist in South Florida.

    Julie Durda WPLG Local 10

    In 2012, Durda left WSVN and joined the WPLG Local 10, the ABC-affiliated TV station, also based in Miami, for which she has been serving as the main weather anchor ever since. Besides this, she has also appeared on several other ABC programs, including “World News” and “Good Morning America”. All these commitments have helped Julie Durda to become one of the leading weather forecasters nationwide, as well as to gain a huge dose of popularity.

    Since 2006, Durda has been a member of the National Weather Association (NWA) while she also holds a license for television weather-casting, approved by the American Meteorological Society (AMS).
